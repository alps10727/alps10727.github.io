1) Abstract

Nominal Network is a mobile-first earning system where users start 8-hour sessions with one button. Referral and in-app boosts can increase the effective rate. No expensive rigs are required; any modern smartphone can participate. The experience is multilingual, device-light, and governed by server time.

  • User-friendly: Simple flows, multilingual UI.
  • Constant baseline: We do not plan to reduce the baseline rate after listing.
  • Device-light: Server validates sessions; device handles UI and notifications.
  • Security-minded: Encrypted transport, strict rules, and anti-abuse controls.

Terminology: “Cloud mining” here does not mean renting PoW/PoS hash power. We use a server-authoritative session model that mints off-chain points which may later convert to NC under announced rules.

2) Problem & Value Proposition

Problem. Traditional crypto mining demands high CAPEX, complex setup, and power usage—limiting access.

Value. Single-tap sessions accrue off-chain points at a stable baseline rate, optionally augmented by referrals and boosts. The device is not taxed; server logic ensures fairness and integrity.

3) Product: Flow & Mechanics

3.1 Basic Flow

  1. Start: User taps Start Mining.
  2. Session: Server records an 8-hour session.
  3. Accrual: Points accrue during the session on the server.
  4. Settlement: Server atomically adds earned points at completion.
  5. Notification: Optional reminders to restart.

3.2 Rate Model

  • Constant baseline for all users; no planned reduction post-listing.
  • Boosts as time-bounded multipliers.
  • Referrals with anti-spam safeguards.

3.3 Device Impact

  • No heavy CPU/GPU workloads.
  • Server timestamps and rules enforce timing and settlement.

6) Security, Privacy & Encryption

  • Transport: TLS-encrypted traffic.
  • Access control: Firestore rules + function validation.
  • Data minimization: Only what’s necessary; GDPR/KVKK principles.
  • Incident handling: Freeze/suspend on abuse; appeal process with auditable logs.

7) Fairness, Anti-Abuse & Single-Account Policy

  • 1 Person = 1 Account. Multi-accounting is prohibited.
  • Device/session validation: Fingerprints, IP/DNS patterns, anomaly scoring.
  • Automation/emulator controls: Root/emulator checks; rapid-tap patterns flagged.
  • Referral anti-spam: Self-referrals, loops, and farms nullified.
  • Sanctions: Warnings → temporary suspension → permanent bans; invalidation for abuse-gained balances.

12) FAQ (Short)

Do I need to keep the phone on for 8 hours? No. Sessions and accrual are enforced by the server; the device mostly starts sessions and shows the UI.

Are points 1:1 with NC? No guarantee. Conversion is proportional and rule-based.

Why KYC? To meet regulatory expectations and promote fairness before listing.

Will the baseline rate drop after listing? Not planned. Sustainability relies on pool allocation and distribution rules.

Roadmap (Target Dates — Draft)

App Launch

First mobile release with core earning features. Single-tap 8-hour sessions, server-authoritative accrual, multilingual UI, and initial boost/referral flows. Baseline security (App Check / Play Integrity), rate limiting, and early observability (start/settlement ratios, error rates, latency).

Community Growth

Scale users and engagement with multilingual content, moderation guidelines, and anti-spam rules. Referral verification flows. Activation/retention cohorts, A/B experiments, community events and creator/influencer partnerships.

Wallet Application

Dedicated wallet for secure token management: account creation/linking, backup/restore, core transfers, notifications. User-friendly seed/key UX; modular chain/network support (final chain TBA).

Wallet Testing

Security and functionality validation: pen-testing, key-management validations, cold/hot storage strategies, recovery scenarios; red-team drills, signature/transaction integrity, incident response, and logging policies.

Token Airdrop

Distribution to early community members and supporters with sybil/multi-account filtering, anti-bot verification, regional constraints, and KYC requirements (where applicable). Point→NC conversion is not 1:1; rules will be published transparently.

Pre-Launch Readiness

Final technical and operational prep: final tokenomics disclosure, independent audits, performance/capacity tests, store compliance reviews, CMP/policy checks, launch campaign and community coordination.

Official Launch

Platform goes live with full feature set: wallet+app integrations, boost/referral v2, notification systems, and operational monitoring. Support channels, FAQs, and appeals are ready; growth experiments and marketing plans are active.

Exchange Listing — Q4 2026 (target)

Listing to improve liquidity and access. Regional eligibility checks, KYC/AML provider integrations, market-making principles, and risk management. Timelines and supported regions will be announced separately.

Desktop CPU “Mining” (Desktop Client)

Desktop app for expanded participation across Windows/macOS/Linux aligned with the server-authoritative session model. Not heavy PoW hashing; focuses on user-friendly flows (UI, notifications, tasks). Extends security and anti-abuse policies to desktop environments.
